Summary

VaultPic is a secure photo vault that protects your private pictures from unauthorized access. It locks your sensitive photos behind fingerprint or face recognition, encrypts them for safety, and works completely offline to ensure privacy. The app includes a discreet camera for taking protected photos and appears as a normal gallery to avoid suspicion, while secretly safeguarding your memories from prying eyes - whether from nosy friends, phone thieves, or hackers.
